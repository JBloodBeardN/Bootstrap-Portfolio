Definitely Not Pokemon

ReadMe

Not Pokemon is a UT coding bootcamp homework activity. While this homework activity was early in the course, and only utilizes HTML, CSS, and JS, it is one of the most complex applications I have created to date. Which, admittedly, may be why it is currently broken, and does not allow play past the first round. I will be refactoring the game soon to unblock the next levels of play, but am still quite proud of the gameplay that currently exists. I am tracking multiple data points for each player character, and made my first attempt at expressing OOP through javascript.

In order to play, select your player from the presented list. Then select the first enemy to fight.

HINT: players have types, and attacks have types. Players who are hit with an attack type to which they are weak (such as a fire player sustaining a water attack), more damage will be done.

After entering 'Battle', select an attack. The application will tell you the result of the attack and your enemies counter attack. Play continues until either player loses all of their health. The game is designed to allow the player to continue taking on enemies, but an error is currently blocking the rest of gameplay. Good Luck, and may the best Not Pokemon Master win!
